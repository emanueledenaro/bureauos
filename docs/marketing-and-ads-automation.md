# Marketing and Ads Automation

BureauOS should grow the owner's business, not only deliver existing work.

The system can plan, draft, create, test, and monitor marketing activity across channels.

## Scope

BureauOS may support:

- X/Twitter posts
- LinkedIn posts
- product announcements
- founder-led updates
- portfolio posts
- case studies
- landing pages
- ad campaign briefs
- image and creative briefs
- audience research
- funnel review
- retargeting plans
- lead capture
- campaign reports

## Social Publishing

Default behavior:

- draft posts autonomously
- create content calendars
- reuse real project proof
- prepare images or creative briefs
- track what was published
- avoid duplicate or unsupported claims

Publishing behavior:

- publish only when explicitly requested or allowed by channel policy
- preserve approval source and scope in memory
- track post URL, date, channel, and outcome

Example approval:

```text
Publish this X thread now.
```

This approves that specific thread only.

## Creative and Image Generation

Creative work should be grounded in real product, client, offer, and brand context.

BureauOS may prepare:

- creative brief
- image prompt
- product mockup brief
- ad visual brief
- brand asset request
- landing page visual direction

Image generation or asset publishing should respect:

- brand guidelines
- client permissions
- platform rules
- copyright and licensing
- claims policy
- privacy constraints

## Ads

Ads are powerful and financially sensitive.

BureauOS may autonomously:

- draft campaign briefs
- draft ad copy
- draft creative concepts
- define audiences
- define landing page requirements
- prepare budget recommendations
- prepare A/B test plans
- monitor campaign performance if connected

BureauOS must ask for approval before:

- launching ads
- increasing budget
- changing live campaign budget
- changing billing/payment settings
- making regulated or unsupported claims
- using client logos or testimonials publicly

## Internal Service Marketing

If the owner has an internal site or service, BureauOS can:

- define the offer
- improve landing pages
- create ad campaigns
- create social content
- run conversion audits
- qualify leads
- draft follow-ups
- prepare proposals

The system should connect marketing to delivery capacity. It should not sell what the delivery team cannot safely deliver.

## Campaign Monitoring

For active campaigns, BureauOS should monitor:

- spend
- leads
- conversion rate
- cost per lead
- quality of leads
- landing page performance
- comments/replies
- approval-sensitive changes

Budget changes and launch actions require explicit approval unless a standing policy exists.
